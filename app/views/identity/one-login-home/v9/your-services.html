Services you can use with your GOV.UK One Login

GOV.UK One Login is new. At the moment, you can only use it to access some government services (for example Personal tax account).

Services you can use with your GOV.UK One Login

You can use GOV.UK One Login with:

  • Apply for a vehicle operator licence
  • Apply to become a registered social worker in England
  • Find a lost teacher reference number (TRN)
  • GOV.UK email subscriptions
  • Licensing for exporting controlled goods (LITE)
  • Personal tax account
  • Recruit an apprentice
  • Register for a National Professional Qualification (NPQ)
  • Register to vote
  • Request a basic DBS check
  • Sign your mortgage deed

GOV.UK One Login does not work with all government accounts and services yet (for example Universal Credit).

In the future, you'll be able to use GOV.UK One Login to access all services on GOV.UK.
