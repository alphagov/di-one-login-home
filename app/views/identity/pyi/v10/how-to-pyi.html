How would you like to prove your identity?

You need to prove your identity before you can apply for a basic DBS check.
You’ll need a photo ID and to answer questions about things like your mobile phone contract, and any bank accounts, credit cards, loans or mortgages you may have.
You’ll need your photo ID and a smartphone with a working camera.
You’ll need to take your photo ID to be checked in person.
