Apply for a basic DBS check

This takes about 15 to 20 minutes.

You’ll be asked to provide personal information, such as:

  • all your addresses for the last 5 years and the dates you lived there
  • your National Insurance number
  • your driving licence (if you have one)

Some of the information you provided as part of proving your identity may be reused so you do not have to give the same details again.
