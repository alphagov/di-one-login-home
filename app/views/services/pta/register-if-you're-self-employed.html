Register if you're self-employed

You’ll need to register for both:

  • Self Assessment
  • Class 2 National Insurance

Register through your tax account .

You’ll need a Government Gateway user ID and password to sign in. If you do not have a user ID, you’ll be able to create one.

When to register

You must tell HMRC by 5 October if you need to send a tax return and you have not sent one before. You could be fined if you do not.

If you cannot register online

Register for Self Assessment and Class 2 National Insurance by in the form online . Print and post it to HMRC.

You’ll need to have all your information ready as you cannot save a partly completed form.

What happens next

You’ll get your Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR) by post in 15 working days (21 days if you’re abroad). You’ll get it sooner in your HMRC app . HMRC will contact you by email or post telling you to complete a Self Assessment tax return before it’s due.
